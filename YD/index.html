Yes Dear

Rock, Country, Folk, Classic Rock

Yes Dear is a 3-piece acoustic band from Nashville, TN that performs a wide range of music. Classic & Southern rock, Country, Folk, Modern Rock....You name it, we can do it. If the Eagles met Van Morrison and went bowling with Jimmy Buffett who was on a 3-way phone call with James Taylor and John Mellencamp backstage at a concert where Tom Petty was opening for the Beatles...that's the Yes Dear sound...

We are now booking corporate events, private parties, fairs, festivals, churches, just about anything. For booking info. please contact us at yesdeartheband@bellsouth.net.
