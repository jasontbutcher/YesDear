If the Eagles met Van Morrison and went bowling with Jimmy Buffett who was on a 3-way phone call with James Taylor and John Mellencamp backstage at a concert where Tom Petty was opening for the Beatles...that's the Yes Dear sound..

Rock, Country, Folk, Classic Rock

Yes Dear is a 3-piece acoustic band from Nashville, TN that performs a wide range of music. Classic & Southern rock, Country, Folk, Modern Rock....You name it, we can do it. We are now booking for 2013. Corporate events, private parties, fairs, festivals, churches, just about anything. For booking info. please contact us at thejjb@bellsouth.net.

Joey Boone

Joey Boone was born and raised in Holly Springs, MS and at the age of sixteen recorded his first album which featured the voices of the legendary Jordonaires. In the 90's, he began working with country hit makers like Bobby Wood (In Another's Eyes) Johnny Christopher (Always on my Mind) and Bobby Emmons (So Much Like my Dad). Joey is a fabulous vocalist, guitarist & pianist who handles much of the lead vocals for Yes Dear.

Josh Jackson

For the last 10 years, Josh Jackson has been known as the lead singer of the widely acclaimed "Josh Jackson Band." In 2002, THE JJB were named in the Nashville Scene Readers Poll as "Best Local Rock Band" & "Best New Band." ROLLING STONE's contributing editor, David Wild, hailed the band as one that "makes great, uplifting rock & roll with a lot of heart and a lot of craft." Josh handles harmony & lead vocals and plays rhythm guitar for Yes Dear.

Locke Sandahl

Locke Sandahl has been performing as lead guitarist with The Josh Jackson Band for the last 10 years and formerly worked with solo artist turned producer, Mitch Dane. (Jars of Clay, Bebo Norman) Locke plays lead guitar and sings harmony vocals and by far has the coolest name of the three of us.
